Leisure Exploration Activity Program (LEAP)

LEAP is the new acronym for ISAP. LEAP stands for Leisure Explore Activity Program and replaces International Student Activity Program. The new name reflects the evolution of the program to be inclusive of all students on campus and when space is available, staff and faculty are welcome to join in.
